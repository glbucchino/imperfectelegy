Meet Max LaBelle. He is a photography major at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. As a junior, Max has begun to build his body of work. He favors film over digital, and has the most experience with black and white photography. He recently has delved into the world of color. Explore his instagram for some new work or get in touch via email.
